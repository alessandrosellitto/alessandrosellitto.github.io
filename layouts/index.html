Alessandro Sellitto

PhD Candidate in Economics
University of Milan & University of Pavia

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Milan and the University of Pavia, and a Research Affiliate at the Düsseldorf Institute for Competition Economics (DICE). My research focuses on digital economics, the economics of AI, industrial organization, and behavioral economics.

I have been a visiting researcher at DICE, the European Commission's Joint Research Centre, and the Toulouse School of Economics.
